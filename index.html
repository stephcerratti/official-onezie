Hailing from Caledonia, Ontario (just outside of Hamilton) Brenden got his start as Onez!e almost three years ago. It wasn't easy for a once hardcore music fan to transition into the electronic scene, and those who have been following him from the beginning will have noticed the many changes in his productions as he tried to find his "sound".

After lots of trial and error, he achieved what he was looking for: blissful and melodic progressive with subtle dark undertones. He doesn't stop there either! Onez!e is such a dynamic producer and draws from many different electronic music styles and always has at least three tracks on the go at once. Onez!e is also quite a talented DJ. He has spun all over Hamilton and is finally breaking into the difficult Toronto scene also.
